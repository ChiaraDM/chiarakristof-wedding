Transportation

Arriving by plane

Getting to the wedding venue from abroad is easiest by plane. The closest airport is: Brindisi Papola Casale Airport (Salento Airport) .

From the airport to your accomodation

If you drive, we recommend you to rent a car from one of the rental companies at the airport: Sixt, Locauto, Autovia, Enterprise Rent-a-car, Hertz.

If you don't drive, you can either prebook a Taxi between the airport and your accomodation for example using: Radio Taxi, Move in Puglia, or just let us know in advance, as there will be a mini bus service rented for some of the guests who don't drive, and we can then arrange the service to be ready when your flight arrives too.

From your accomodation to the wedding venues

If you drive, there's plenty of parking space both near the church in Galataina, for example around Villa Comunale and also at the entrance of Cala dei Balacani: (40.038093, 18.456934).

If you don't drive, again you have to option to prebook a Taxi, or let us know to see if we could arrange the mini bus service to pick you up / drop you off at the locations.
